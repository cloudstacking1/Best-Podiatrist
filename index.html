Why You Need a Podiatrist

A podiatrist is a medical specialist focused on diagnosing and treating conditions related to the feet, ankles, and lower extremities. Podiatrists undergo extensive training, including medical school and specialized residency programs, to provide expert care for a wide range of foot-related issues. Regular visits to a podiatrist can help you maintain optimal foot health and prevent more serious problems from developing.

    Understanding Bunions and Their Treatment

    Bunions are bony bumps that form on the joint at the base of your big toe. They occur when some of the bones in the front part of your foot move out of place, causing the tip of your big toe to be pulled towards the smaller toes. This misalignment causes the joint at the base of your big toe to stick out, resulting in pain, swelling, and difficulty walking.
